Am I elligible to join?

As we are affiliated with San Jose State University (SJSU), we primarily are looking for students who attend San Jose State University. However, we have several athletes who are from different universities and alumni working locally that train with us.

I cant swim! Will I be safe during practice?

Dragon boat is a water sport, and you may get wet during practices. However, we take protection of our athletes very seriously and require our athletes to wear life jackets at all times. You will not need the ability to swim

Do I need prior experiece?

Though prior experience can help, you do not need to have any paddling experience to join us! Currently, we have members who are novices with no experience as well as members who are part of the United States national collegiate team

Where do practices take place, and how often will I need to practice?

We currently have water practice at Redwood City, which is a 25 minute drive from SJSU. We are planning on eventually shifting to Lake Vasona, which is much more local to SJSU. We practice Sunday mornings on the water, but actively promote our members working out in "workout pods" during the week

How often will we be competing?

Given the current pandemic, our opportunities to compete are much more sparse than prior years. However we still have plans to compete at races in 2022. Races generally occur during weekends, but athletes busy with accademics and other commitments are not required to attend all races
